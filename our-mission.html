Our Mission

‘Pick of the Market’ is a scheme to make food accessible through our “pay-as-you-feel” pricing structure, and help the environment by rescuing excess stock from local food distributors.

We hope to cultivate a community of environmentally-conscious food-lovers who love to try something new and support each other.

If you’d like to support our mission, we are currently seeking volunteers for a variety of different roles, including chefs, waiters and warehouse staff.
