Directions

Situated in Filton’s Abbey Wood Shopping Park, our restaurant has excellent public transport connections via the m1, 70 and 73 bus routes, as well as being just a 10-minute walk from Bristol Abbey Wood train station and a 15-minute walk from UWE Frenchay campus.
