What Do We Do?

Whether you’re looking for a family day out or a cozy night in, we’re here to take the stress out of picking a delicious meal.

Every evening, our dedicated staff and volunteers visit local food distributors to divert their excess stock straight from the shelves to your hands. Not only is this a huge environmental win, it means we can offer diverse, delicious meals to our patrons regardless of their budget.

That’s right, when you dine with us, or order one of our carefully crafted food boxes, you decide the price!

So, why not come and take a pick?
